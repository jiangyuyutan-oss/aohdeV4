## Important
Do not copy all GameValues files into your mod.
Only copy the files you actually modify.

This allows other modders to edit and override the remaining files.

If you copy all .json files from:
modsExamples/gameValues_AllFiles/

into:
game/gameValues/

You will block other mods from modifying those files.

For example, if a player uses two or more mods, copying all GameValues files may override changes made by other modders.
